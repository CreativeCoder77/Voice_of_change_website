Vision 2047 : Goals & Strategic Blueprint

Economic Prosperity

  • Goal: Reach a $10 trillion economy.
  • Focus: Widespread economic opportunities and growth across all sectors.

Technological Advancement

  • Goal: Position India as a global innovation hub.
  • Focus: Leadership in AI, digital solutions, and cutting-edge technologies.

Sustainable Development

  • Goal: Environmental sustainability for future generations.
  • Focus: Green energy, efficient waste management, and conservation efforts.

Social Equity

  • Goal: Eradicate poverty and reduce inequality.
  • Focus: Universal access to education, healthcare, and housing.

Global Leadership

  • Goal: Enhance India's role on the world stage.
  • Focus: Strengthened diplomacy, global trade, and peace initiatives.

Strategic Blueprint

  • To realize these goals, the strategic blueprint includes:

Economic Reforms

  • Policies: Boost industrial growth, support startups, and simplify doing business.

Infrastructure Development

  • Expansion: Modernize transportation, urban planning, and digital connectivity.

Education & Skill Development

  • Revamp: Prioritize STEM, vocational training, and continuous learning.

Healthcare

  • Focus: Universal access, preventive care, and advanced research.

Environmental Policies

  • Initiatives: Renewable energy, afforestation, water conservation, and carbon reduction.

Governance Reforms

  • Enhancements: Improve transparency, reduce bureaucracy, and digitize public services.

Progress Monitoring

  • Annual Reports: Detailed updates on initiative progress.
  • Performance Indicators: Metrics like GDP, employment, literacy, and emissions.
  • Public Dashboards: Real-time data on critical projects for transparency and accountability.

This streamlined framework is designed to guide India's transformation into a global powerhouse by 2047, ensuring a prosperous, sustainable, and inclusive future.
