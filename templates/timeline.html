India's Progress Over Time.

Discover

↓

2000-2005

Building the Digital Backbone

  • National Informatics Centre (NIC) Expansion: Enhanced government digital infrastructure.
  • ICT in Schools: Introduction of computer literacy programs in schools.
  • Public Internet Access: Expansion of internet access in rural and urban areas.
2006-2010

Green and Clean India

  • National Solar Mission: Launched to promote solar energy as part of the National Action Plan on Climate Change.
  • Clean India Campaign (Swachh Bharat): Initiatives to improve sanitation and cleanliness nationwide.
  • Forest Conservation Act Enforcement: Strengthened policies for increasing forest cover and biodiversity conservation.
2011-2015

Digital and Urban Innovation

  • Digital India Initiative: Launched to enhance digital infrastructure, increase internet penetration, and promote e-governance.
  • Smart Cities Mission (Phase 1): Identified cities for transformation into smart cities with a focus on sustainable living and efficient public services.
  • National Skill Development Mission: Emphasized skill development to prepare the workforce for emerging industries.
2016-2020

Technology and Sustainability Drive

  • Digital India 2.0: Expansion of digital services, enhanced cybersecurity, and nationwide digital literacy programs.
  • Green India Mission: Aimed at increasing forest cover, promoting renewable energy, and reducing pollution levels.
  • AI and Robotics Investments: Government and private sector investments in AI research and robotics to foster innovation and integrate these technologies into industries.
2021-2023

Health and Urban Transformation

  • Healthcare Innovation: Major advances in genomics, vaccine development, and personalized medicine to improve public health.
  • Smart Cities Project (Phase 2): Continued development of over 100 smart cities, integrating modern amenities, efficient public services, and sustainable living conditions.
  • National Digital Health Mission: Launched to create a digital health ecosystem, providing secure and accessible healthcare data for all citizens.
2024-2029

Scaling Innovation and Infrastructure

  • Digital India 3.0: Further expansion of digital infrastructure, including 5G rollout nationwide, enhancement of AI and machine learning capabilities, and ensuring cybersecurity resilience.
  • Green Energy Revolution: Transition to renewable energy sources, with major investments in solar, wind, and hydroelectric power, targeting significant reductions in carbon emissions.
  • Smart Cities Project (Phase 3): Completion of smart city development, with an emphasis on sustainable urban planning, waste management, and green public transportation systems.
  • Advanced AI and Robotics: Integration of AI and robotics into agriculture, manufacturing, and public services, driving productivity and innovation.
2030-2035

Transforming Healthcare and Education

  • Healthcare Revolution: Expansion of personalized medicine, AI-driven diagnostics, and telemedicine services, aiming for universal healthcare access.
  • Education Overhaul: Complete digitization of education, with a focus on STEM, vocational training, and continuous learning programs to prepare citizens for a knowledge-based economy.
  • AI Governance: Implementation of AI in governance to streamline public services, enhance policy-making, and improve citizen engagement.
2036-2040

Sustainable Development and Global Leadership

  • Environmental Restoration: Large-scale projects for reforestation, water conservation, and pollution control to ensure environmental sustainability.
  • Global Technology Hub: Establishment of India as a global leader in technology and innovation, particularly in AI, robotics, and green tech.
  • Global Partnerships: Strengthening of international collaborations in trade, technology, and environmental initiatives, enhancing India's global influence.
2041-2047

Realizing Viksit Bharat

  • Smart Nation: Transition from smart cities to a smart nation, where all aspects of life, including governance, health, education, and infrastructure, are interconnected and optimized through digital technologies.
  • Green Bharat: Achieving carbon neutrality, with a fully sustainable energy grid, zero waste cities, and nationwide conservation programs.
  • Inclusive Prosperity: Eradication of poverty, with every citizen having access to quality healthcare, education, housing, and economic opportunities, fulfilling the vision of an inclusive and prosperous India.
